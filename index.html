Hello Lovelies! I want to show you guys how you can take your summer maxi and use it for fall.



We all love a good maxi and it is such a versatile piece that you can wear even when the weather starts changing. Once it starts getting a little more chilly don't put that maxi dress away with the rest of your summer clothes. You can easily wear some tights and throw on some booties.
